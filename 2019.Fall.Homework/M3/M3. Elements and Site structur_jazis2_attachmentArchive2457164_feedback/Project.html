Mount Kenya Cabin

Located near the Lake Michaelson, these cabins were smartly designed based on the Biophilic principles,
which are the visual connection with nature and the presence of water.Four fundamental ideas are at
the core of the design: site-specific, quirky, openness and environmental-friendly cabins are to make
sure the visitors to get the most enjoyable experiences at Mount Kenya during their stay.

The Goods Hideout

A temporary installation was designed to gain life in this deserted carpark.
This place lacks social interaction despite having lots of access to its location.
The design responds to the lifestyle of the community in Brunswick in the context of
creating a better environment. The concept is a moveable glass-house like structure
that provides numerous opportunities for the community to get involved in this temporary
installation through some activities, such as planting herbs workshop during the day,
pop up cinema at night and seasonal events on the weekend.

Munchies at The Folly

Munchies at the Folly situated near the RMIT Brunswick is designed to represent Maker and Monger Retail shop.
The idea behind is to bring back the childhood memories through food and interior space. A new exciting eating
experience, using the form of a playground, has been introduced to this kiosk with attempt pushing people to
eat together and making conversation, while enjoying themselves in this new playing experience for the adult.
