ABOUT

Born and raised in the metropolitan city, Jakarta, made
Esther develop a keen eye for design. Thus, she has enjoyed various
aspects of creating art ever since. She is currently studying
graphic design at the Academy of Arts University in San Francisco.
She hopes to start a career in the field she has long been passionate in.

Besides her love for art, she also enjoys photography, playing sports,
watching movies and listening to music. Additionally, she is a solemn believer
of the work-life balance as one of the keys to achieve success.
